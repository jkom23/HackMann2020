NASA vs Aliens: Driver's Seat

About our Game

As fans of the original arcade games Galaga and Space Invaders, we wanted to create a modern adaptation of the game in 3D. The original 8-bit games in 2D, so we decided to jump into the future with high-resolution graphics and detailed models to create a beautiful looking 3D game. Further, we wanted to create a more immersive experience, and that is why we created the "Driver's Seat" mode. This choice puts the player in the front seat of the game, rather than being a 3rd-person observer. Finally, we added a modern twist to the game, by adding a N.A.S.A. theme to the space ships as they battle alien invaders!

Gameplay

Players can control their space shuttle using their keyboard, and the smooth graphics of UE4 allow for an intuitive, immersive, and, most importantly, fun gameplay experience. Below is a video captured by Jack in Unreal Engine 4 displaying the gameplay and graphics.
